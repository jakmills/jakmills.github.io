About Me

Hello! I'm Jake Miller, a Computer Science Student with a passion for creating software. I am currently a Junior at Rowan University. I am currently interested in becoming a software engineer or cybersecurity specialist. Both of these fields are interesting to me. I have always been interested in computers and technology.
